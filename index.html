Constructions

Construction transport is the movement of objects such as rubble, tools or asphalt from one place to another by means of vehicles

Road Infrastructure Development

Building and maintaining roads is crucial for economic growth and connectivity. Modern road construction involves using advanced materials and techniques to ensure durability and efficiency.

Public Transit Systems

Urban public transit systems, including buses, trams, and subways, reduce congestion and pollution. Construction projects focus on extending routes, increasing frequency, and upgrading infrastructure

Port and Harbor Development

Development projects include expanding docking facilities, improving cargo handling systems, and integrating advanced logistics technologies. Efficient ports boost economic growth.

Sustainable Transport Solutions

Sustainable transport focuses on reducing environmental impact through green construction practices and renewable energy use. Projects include building bike lanes, pedestrian paths, and electric vehicle charging stations.

Objective

  • Use a strategic approach in selecting projects that will meet the Annual Business Plan goals;
  • Develop and maintain an Estimating Management System;
  • Be selective in choosing our customers and type of projects;
  • Ensure that GMT has the ability to meet our customers’ requirements;
  • Select suppliers and subcontractors based on their ability to meet our customers’ requirements;
